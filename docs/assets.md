# Assets

## Hero Image Direction

The hero image should be cinematic, personal and premium.

Target:
- Yusuf Dere
- dark hoodie or minimal dark clothing
- seen from behind or 3/4 profile
- looking at city lights, sea or skyline
- evening or night atmosphere
- Samsun-like coastal city feeling
- not looking directly at camera
- calm, cinematic, timeless

Use the uploaded personal photos only as identity and pose references.

Use:
- first photo for profile and face reference
- second photo for face, hair and hoodie reference
- fourth photo for back/shoulder composition reference

Avoid:
- cigarette
- alcohol signs
- bathroom mirror
- flash photo style
- influencer pose
- overedited look
