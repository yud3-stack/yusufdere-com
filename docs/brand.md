# YusufDere.com Brand

## Brand Name

Yusuf Dere

## Core Line

Building the life I imagined.

## Location

Samsun, Türkiye

## Social

Instagram: @heyyud3

## Brand Goal

YusufDere.com is the central hub of Yusuf Dere's personal brand.

The website should not feel like a CV, resume, agency site or corporate portfolio.

It should feel like a premium personal operating system where visitors can discover:
- who Yusuf is
- what he is building
- what he is thinking
- what he is photographing
- what he is currently focused on
- what projects he is working on

## Brand Personality

- focused
- minimal
- ambitious
- calm
- technical
- cinematic
- personal
- future-oriented

## Avoid

- CV-style layout
- corporate tone
- generic portfolio template
- excessive gradients
- flashy animations
- trendy design that will age badly
- fake luxury
- cluttered sections
